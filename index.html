This repl is made just for testing. It can automatically capture users' photographs even without alerting. It will upload image to the backend of the repl.

The index is safe. A console can generate capture links, but it is just a developed demo. Don't use it to capture others' photos. Other pages may try to capture users.

I don't mean to steal replers' photos. Please don't give any camera permission if you don't want be photographed.
